WaaS Frequently Asked Questions

What is Website as a Service (WaaS)?

WaaS is an all-inclusive subscription model. Instead of paying thousands of euros upfront for design and development, you pay a flat monthly fee. We build, host, secure, and update the site for you.

What is the commitment period for Option 2 (WaaS)?

Option 2 is built on a minimum contract of 12 or 24 months. If you wish to terminate early, the website remains the property of TheGenAI, or you can purchase it for the standard one-off development price (€499).

How does the WhatsApp Content Support work?

It's our killer support feature! If you finish a project, renovate a house, or paint a room, just send us the photos and a brief description directly on WhatsApp. We will format them and upload them beautifully to your website portfolio section within 24 hours.

Are the websites optimized for Google and AI search engines?

Absolutely. Every site we build is highly optimized using Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) and standard SEO practices. This ensures search engines and AI assistants (like ChatGPT or Claude) easily read, cite, and recommend your services.
